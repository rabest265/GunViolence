The chart above compares number of gun incidents per population size in states with laws restricting gun access to the benchmark number for the nation including all states. In some cases, selecting restrictions from the menus above shows a decrease in gun incidents/100M people in states that apply those restrictions, as one might expect. In many other cases though, the number of incidents shows a dramatic increase in restrictive states, such as Connecticut, which is visible when all of the most limiting choices are selected above. This may indicate that high numbers of gun incidents have caused certain states to enact more restrictive laws, or that there are other strong factors influencing gun violence rates.
